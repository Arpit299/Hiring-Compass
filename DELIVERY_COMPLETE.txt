## üéâ What Was Delivered

A **premium motion-driven narrative experience** that transforms resume analysis into an interactive scroll story. Users scroll through 5 carefully choreographed stages of insights as animations fade/slide/scale based on scroll position (not time).

### The Experience
```
User uploads resume
         ‚Üì
   [Sticky header appears]
         ‚Üì
Scroll ‚Üí Stage 1: Resume Intake (files, processing)
Scroll ‚Üí Stage 2: ATS Score Breakdown (metrics, ratings)

Scroll ‚Üí Stage 4: Market Pulse (demand, salary, skills, companies)
Scroll ‚Üí Stage 5: 30-Day Improvement Roadmap (milestones, metrics)
Scroll ‚Üí CTA Button: "Start Your Plan"
```

**Psychology:** Linear scroll + progressive reveals = higher trust + perceived AI sophistication

## üé® Animation Design

| Animation | Trigger | Duration | Easing |
|-----------|---------|----------|--------|
| **Fade** | Scroll into viewport | 0.6s | cubic-out |
| **Slide Up** | Scroll into viewport | 0.6s | cubic-out |
| **Scale (0.95‚Üí1)** | Scroll into viewport | 0.6s | cubic-out |
| **Stagger** | Per-item delay | 0.1-0.15s | sequential |
| **Progress Bar** | Page scroll depth | Linear | 0‚Äì100% |

**Key:** All animations mapped to scroll position (not time), so users control pace.
